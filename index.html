About Us

Comoros Future Builders (CFB) is a youth-led organization dedicated to inspiring and equipping young people with the skills and knowledge they need to shape the future of our islands. We believe in the power of education, technology, and creativity to build a stronger, more sustainable Comoros.

Our Programs

Tech for Youth

Workshops and training sessions to help young people learn coding, web design, and digital literacy.

Education Support

Providing mentorship and resources to help students achieve academic success and career readiness.

Innovation Labs

Encouraging creativity and problem-solving through community-based projects and hackathons.
